Project 3

To All The Girls Organization Article

An online article about social-impact within the realm of feminism.

Writing Content Social Media

Overview

This piece was created for Instagram, in order to reach more students whose online precense are visible. Thus, the challenge was making the message meaningful and easy to read quickly. I focused on a strong hook, clear structure, and a tone that feels human.

Process

  • Started with a hook that captures attention in the first second
  • Broke information into short, readable sections
  • Kept the content skimmable without losing the message’s emotional impact
