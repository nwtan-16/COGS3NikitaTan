Project 2

Stock Pitch Competition Deck (SIG)

A polished slide deck designed to communicate an investment thesis clearly and confidently.

Slides Storytelling Visual Design

Overview

For SIG’s Stock Pitch Competition, I contributed to building a deck that communicates key points quickly. Altogether, my teammates and I were able to deliver our thesis, evidence, catalysts, risks and recommendation within the alloted time while keeping the design clean and consistent for live presenting.

Process

  • Organized the narrative so the pitch flows logically from Background → Thesis → Financial modeling → Final Recommendation
  • Simplified slides and highlighted key numbers so judges can scan quickly
  • Kept layouts consistent (fonts, spacing, hierarchy) to reduce distraction during presenting
